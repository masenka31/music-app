{{ title }} App

An app that let's you find music based on your taste. Have a playlist you want to expand but feeling tired to browse millions of songs? We've got you covered. Just submit your playlist and our algorithm will do the rest for you!

What is it about?

Say you are tired of listening to the same songs over and over again. You want something fresh, something new, something you've never heard before. That's where our app comes in. Based on powerfull machine learning algorithms we can recommend songs you'll like but probably didn't come across yet.

Implemented models

We have four different models you can choose from. Random model is well... random. KNN model finds users with similar tastes as you. Word2Vec tries to find associations between your chosen songs and songs you could like. And ALS model...
